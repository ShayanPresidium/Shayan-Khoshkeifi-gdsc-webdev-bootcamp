Persian Foods

Delicious Persian Foods

Chelo Kebab

Chelo Kebab is one of the most famous Persian dishes, consisting of saffron rice served with grilled meat kebabs. It is often accompanied by grilled tomatoes and butter.

Ghormeh Sabzi

Ghormeh Sabzi is a flavorful stew made with fresh herbs, kidney beans, and lamb. It is typically served with Persian rice.

Fesenjan

Fesenjan is a rich and tangy stew made with ground walnuts, pomegranate molasses, and chicken or duck. Its sweet and sour taste is unforgettable.
